and **the swarm** (a Kimi K3 review swarm, the outside eye), couriered by the Master.

holding an exam. Nothing in between was wasted.

## What it built

A bounded gate loop with a real `verifying → executing` back-edge — the order
lifecycle is a cycle, not a straight line with two sinks. Keep-best, proven against
regression. Two walls (temporal + contextual) that make the exam untouchable. A
measuring stick that knows its own resolution and refuses to arm on thin poison. An
untouchable build gate that arms and blocks offline on executable truth. A planner
that authors the exam for served orders — the LAW OF EXTERNAL FITNESS turned on the
exam-writer itself. And a record cat-able all the way down, including the night it
recorded its own blank exam and told the truth about it.

The judge sits with an exam in hand now.
